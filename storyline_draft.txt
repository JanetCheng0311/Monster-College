Title: Monster College Competition Day

Characters:
Principal Sir Doggegg : A bespectacled dog.
Student Athlete Flying Tiger: A tiger capable of flight.
Student Athlete Max: A giant mechanical lizard.

Scene One: Inside the College

Narrator: This is a monster College where pupils are divided into two main categories: magical creatures and mechanical beasts. Each year, they hold competitions to test their combat prowess.

Principal Sir Doggegg: (Anxiously) Oh dear! This year's competition is about to begin, but the magical team is short one creature! What shall we do?

Scene Two: In the Garden

(Whoosh! Flying Tiger streaks low over everyone's heads.)

Principal Sir Doggegg: (Surprised) Blimey! That's Flying Tiger! Flying so fast—truly a prodigy!

(The headmaster runs over)

Principal Sir Doggegg: Flying Tiger, we need you! Will you join the tournament?

Flying Tiger: (Landing on the ground, pondering) Hmm... Alright! I'm in!

Scene Three: Tournament Arena

(On the stage stands a mechanical lizard, Max, its entire body gleaming with a metallic sheen.)

Max: I am Max. I shall defeat you.

Flying Tiger: Hmph! I'm not afraid of you!

(Principal Sir Doggegg, wearing large spectacles, stands beside the ring filming with a camera.)

Principal Sir Doggegg: I am the referee! On your marks—get set—go!

(Max charges forward; Flying Tiger soars into the air!)

Narrator: To be continued...
